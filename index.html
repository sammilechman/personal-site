About

Hello! I am a web developer currently residing in North Beach, San Francisco. I received my bachelor's from UCLA (Biophysics 2011) and spent several years working in the medical field before I decided to become a web developer.

What draws me to coding? For me, it's the constant challenge, the feeling of solving a complex problem simply and efficiently, and the unlimited body of knowledge to learn. I love to code.

They say that as you learn a new skill, you fluctuate between a state of frustration and satisfaction as your brain adapts to the challenge. I think the key to becoming a great programmer is to never let yourself stay in one state for too long.

I have experience in the following technologies: Ruby, Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, Backbone.js, SQL, Git, HTML, CSS, PostgreSQL, and Heroku.

You can find me on the web at: LinkedIn, GitHub and Facebook.
